Known for her distinctive style and appearance Grace Jones is a singer, model and actress originally from Spanish Town, Jamaica.

Appearing on the catwalks of Kenzo and Yves St Laurent, she has also graced the covers of numerous mag's including Vogue, Elle and Playboy, starred in A View To A Kill - James Bond and famously posed for iconic photographs taken by John Paul Goude and Guy Bourdin to name a few.

Over the years she has released 10 studio albums, working with a range of artists and producers including Sly and Robbie (the famous reggae Rhythm twins), Trevor Horn and Trickie. Notable singles include Pull Up To The Bumper, My Jamaican Guy, Slave To The Rhythm, Private Life and William's Blood.

In 2015 she released her autobiography entitled Grace Jones, I'll never write my memoirs and continues to inspire and perform today.
